About the Photographer

Hello! I'm Abdul Malik, Adeyanju Lawal, A Professional Photographer based in Lagos with over 7 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey with photography began when I was just a teenager, fascinated by how a single image could tell a powerful story. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a lifelong passion and eventually a fulfilling career.

I specialize in portrait, landscape, and event photography, bringing a unique perspective to each shoot. My style combines classic techniques with modern aesthetics to create timeless images that you'll treasure for years to come. I've had the privilege of working with amazing clients across the country, from intimate family portraits to large-scale commercial projects.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new locations, studying the work of photography masters, or experimenting with new techniques to bring to my next session.

My Equipment

I primarily shoot with Sony Alpha series cameras and a selection of prime lenses that help me achieve the look and feel I'm known for.
